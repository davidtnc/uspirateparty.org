date: '2014-04-28 20:06:08'

Lindsay-Anne Brunner, Chairperson of the United States Pirate Party, will be taking a medical leave starting tomorrow, Tuesday, April 29th, 2014 continuing for the duration of the month of May.

During this time, Andrew Norton, Vice-Chairperson of the United States Pirate Party, will be assuming her duties until she returns and should be contacted for any and all inquiries for United States Pirate Party related matters.
